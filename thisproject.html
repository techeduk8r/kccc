Project Background

The Kitty City Clicker Club was inspired by the Assistant Director of Feline Behavior's participation in the Jackson Galaxy Cat Pawsitive Pro program. In a collaborative effort, the Assistant Director and I worked to develop a website that would provide clicker training volunteers and fosters with resources to improve the lives of cats in our care.

This project consisted of designing a logo based on the Assistant Director's concept, creating on brand media elements and videos, instructor led and virtual instructor led training materials, and an accessible resoucse site for volunteers and fosters.

Project Details

  • Type: Logo, Training Videos, Website, ILT/VILT Training Materials
  • Date: October 2021
  • Client: PAWS Chicago

Project Attributes

  • Logo Design - Adobe Illustrator
  • HTML/CSS
  • Video Production - Camtasia
